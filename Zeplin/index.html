The African
Grocery Store!

At MTB Halal Market, we are passionate about
bringing the vibrant flavors and rich culinary
traditions of West Africa to your doorstep.

Our store is a treasure

Trove of authentic ingredients, carefully sourced from various regions across West Africa. From the aromatic spices and exotic herbs to the staple grains and traditional condiments, we offer an extensive selection of products that will transport you to the heart of West Africa with every bite.

Supporting local farmers & suppliers

We take pride in supporting local farmers and suppliers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. By forging partnerships with small-scale producers, we ensure that our products are not only delicious but also contribute to the economic growth and well-being of West African communities.

Stepping into our store

you will be greeted by the warm smiles of our knowledgeable staff, who are always ready to assist you in finding the perfect ingredients for your West African-inspired meals. Whether you are a seasoned cook or new to West African cuisine, we are here to guide you, share our expertise, and help you explore the vast array of flavors that this incredible region has to offer.

wide range of grocery items

n addition to our wide range of grocery items, we also offer freshly prepared foods, including traditional dishes and snacks. Our in-house kitchen is dedicated to creating authentic West African delicacies, made with love and care. From jollof rice to suya, puff puff to fufu, we invite you to indulge in the taste sensations that have captivated generations.
